

# MindFleet Distributor OS - Landing Page

## Overview
Build a visually striking landing page for "MindFleet Distributor OS" inspired by the reference design, with a split-layout hero section and two call-to-action buttons.

## Layout & Design

### Left Panel (Purple gradient background)
- **Brand header**: MindFleet logo + "DISTRIBUTOR OS" subtitle
- **Hero headline**: "The future of supply chain AI is here."
- **Three feature highlights** with icons:
  - **AI-Driven Logistics** — Optimize routes, manage stock, and predict demand with real-time intelligence.
  - **Enterprise Security** — Role-based access, encrypted logs, and industrial-grade security as standard.
  - **Global Scale** — Built for massive distribution networks across regional and urban centers.

### Right Panel (White/light background)
- **Two large CTA buttons** (replacing the login/profile section):
  1. **"Demo of the Product"** — Opens a modal/dialog saying "🚀 Launching Soon! We're putting the finishing touches on our interactive demo. Stay tuned!"
  2. **"Product Overview"** — Opens a modal/dialog saying "🚀 Launching Soon! Our comprehensive product overview is on its way. Check back soon!"
- **Footer text**: "MINDFLEET AI" on the left, green "● SECURE" badge on the right

## Interactions
- Both buttons open a clean dialog/modal with a "Launching Soon" message and a close button
- Buttons have hover effects for a polished feel
- Responsive: stacks vertically on mobile

## Visual Style
- Deep purple-to-indigo gradient on the left panel
- Clean white right panel
- Purple/green accent colors matching the reference
- Modern, professional typography
